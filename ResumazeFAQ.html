General questions

How does this process work?

First, visit our “Pricing” on the home page and pick your professional resume package. You will then be prompted to upload your resume to our Dropbox then redirected to a quick questionnaire which is necessary for us to get a better idea of your work history, accomplishments and career goals. Be sure to copy and paste sample job descriptions of positions or opportunity you are targeting. Then you submit the payment, sit back and relax.

Once we receive all your information, your expert resume writer will reach out to you with any questions he/she may have if needed. Within 1 -2 business days from your initial order, you will receive your brand new documents via email. If you have any edits, changes or questions, they may be discussed via email at that time. Once all documents are finalized, you will receive final versions and a free gift to prepare you for all the job interviews that will be coming your way.

How long does this process take?

Once we have received payment, your completed questionnaire and a current copy of your current résumé, it will take 1 - 2 business days for you to receive the final draft. At that point you have 7 days to respond with any edits you may have and can discuss any questions with us at that time. We hardly have a large number of edits from our clients so the entire process typically takes less than 1 week.

Do you offer refunds?

We strive for perfection with each and every service we provide and pride ourselves on being the best resume writing service for college students online. If you are not 100% satisfied with the final copy of any document or website, we will continue to revise it until you are completely confident that your self-marketing tools are a true representation of you! Thus, we do not offer refunds.
